About

I am an Assistant Professor at UNINT, and co-founder of both the Prometheus Laboratory and the Sapienza University startup 4AI, where I supervise projects in artificial intelligence and computer vision. After earning my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Sapienza University of Rome, I contributed to the VisionLab for nearly a decade, leading and supporting projects in event recognition, object detection, person re-identification, AR/VR, robotics, and signal processing. My work has been applied in diverse domains, from drone-based aerial surveillance and underwater imaging to biomedical signal analysis and humanâ€“computer interaction. I have served as Principal Investigator and work package leader in several national research projects, published widely in top journals and conferences, and acted as reviewer and guest editor for international venues. I am also an NVIDIA Deep Learning Institute Ambassador and an active member of professional organizations such as IEEE, ACM, IAPR, and EurAI.
