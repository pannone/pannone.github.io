About

Hi there#

I’m Daniele Pannone, and I got my M.Sc. (2015) and Ph.D. (2018) in Computer Science from Sapienza University of Rome. From 2015 to 2024, I was part of the Computer Vision Laboratory (VisionLab) at the Department of Computer Science. Since 2020, I’ve been an Assistant Professor at the same Department. In 2024, I co-founded both the Prometheus Lab and 4AI, a startup at Sapienza focused on pushing the boundaries of artificial intelligence. That same year, I also became an NVidia Deep Learning University Ambassador. My research covers a lot of topics, including machine and deep learning, event recognition, object detection, person re-identification, signal analysis and processing, bioinformatics, human-computer interaction, and robotics.

I’m also a member of several professional organizations, including IAPR, CVPL, IEEE, ACM, AIxIA, and EurAI.
